Enterprise introduction
     Yibin Goldway Chemical Technology Co., Ltd. is located in Yangchun Industrial Park, Jiangan County, Yibin City, Sichuan Province.
      The company was founded in 2013 and has become the well-known barium chloride manufacturer in the country. Our company is mainly engaged in chemical production, sales, import and export trade in one comprehensive enterprise. The quality of barium salt series products produced by the company has reached the domestic standards of similar products, and the products are sold well in China, Europe, America and Asia-Pacific region.
      Here, Goldway, together with all the colleagues, sincerely hopes to cooperate with more friends in the world and seek common development.
